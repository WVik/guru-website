FAQs

  • Q1: Which direction should we organize the Puja?
    A1: East-West. You can face any direction.
  • Q2: How long does it take for the Puja?
    A2: We require 30 minutes for the initial set up. You can see time as per puja below:
    Puja Time
    Vastu Shanti 3 hours
    Satyanarayan 2 hours
    Munj 3 hours
    Wedding 3 hours
    Sod-munj 2:30 hours
    Gruha pravesh 1:30 hours
    Ganesh puja 1 hour
    Ganapati Pran Pratishthapana 1 hour
    Engagement 2 hours
    Grahamakh (separate) 2 hours
  • Q3: Where do I find the things required for the Puja?
    A3: From the list shared, we bring in non-perishable items. The things required from your end are written as Yajman under the Arranger column. You can get these in any Indian grocery store.
